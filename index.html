Work

Actualize Coding Bootcamp

Inspired by my peers and the tech space I worked in, I applied and was accepted into the Actualize program. Over the 12-week intensive course, I learned the fundamentals of programming and developing web applications with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VueJS, and Ruby on Rails.

Tripper

For my capstone project, I built Tripper. This app allows users to efficiently plan trips by creating trip folders and adding points of interests, such as hotels, restaurants, attractions, cultural institutions, they want to visit on their respective trips. Tripper was built using Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, VueJS, HTML, CSS, and Google Places, Maps, and Geocoding APIs.

Events and Communications at Audible

Audible is the leading digital provider of the spoken word. At Audible, I've partnered with different teams to plan and produce company-wide events, conferences, and meetings. I transitioned to a communications role, I collaborate with teams to plan, strategize, and deliver internal communications to our Audible community.
